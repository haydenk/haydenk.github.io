## Notes

* **Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D. S6-7** (2019-2020) diverge from the main MCU timeline entirely (ignoring the Blip). Watch them separately after the main timeline if interested. Available on **Disney+**.
* **Loki** (both seasons) and **What If...?** (all seasons) exist largely outside the main timeline. They can be watched in release order at any point after Endgame.
* Shows previously on Netflix (Daredevil, Jessica Jones, Luke Cage, Iron Fist, The Defenders, The Punisher) have all moved to **Disney+**.
* **Spider-Man** films (Homecoming, Far From Home, No Way Home) are Sony co-productions. Availability on Disney+ varies by region -- they may require purchase/rental on digital platforms in some areas.
* Canon status is debated for Cloak & Dagger, Runaways, and Inhumans, but they are included for completeness.
* Animated series (Your Friendly Neighborhood Spider-Man, Marvel Zombies, Eyes of Wakanda) are set in alternate timelines, not the main MCU continuity.
* In-universe years are approximate -- Marvel has shifted dates multiple times. Placements reflect the most current consensus.
